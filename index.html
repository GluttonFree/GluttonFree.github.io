Problem statement: USC Gym Population

Too many people use the USC gym at one time.

Affinity Diagram: Gym Population Problem

The Gym a lot of the time has too many people using it at once for people to get the full benefit of it. The equipment is always being used by someone else. We are coming up with ways to remedy that issue.

Persona: Meili Leyte

Meili here is a typical user of the gym. She suffers from the overpopulation of the gym. She actively tries to spend her spare time on her workout routine but cannot because of the people in the gym.

Benefits of the App

Here we have some individuals wondering if they should go to the gym. They only want to go when it isn't overpopulated since they want to be able to make the most of their time there. They check the app to see if there is a small amount of people there.

Sketch of the App

The app requires you to sign-in or log-in with an email account and password. Once you do that, you will be presented with gyms in your general area. If you click on one, it will show how many people are there in the. The amount of traffic in and out of the gym, and how much time left til it closes.
